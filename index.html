Welcome to Harmony Villa!
Welcome to Harmony Villa Kissamos, you own personal seaside escape, with its own private beach at your feet! This unique property is the only one in Crete offering access to a small cove exclusively at your disposal, just for you and your loved ones to enjoy, combined with mesmerising sea views and absolute privacy. Situated just 2 km away from the lively area of Kissamos, the Villa also offers easy access to all necessary daily amenities such as restaurants, cafes, shops, health centre, supermarkets, bank, post office and more. Finding Harmony Villas Kissamos will be easy for you, but leaving it might be harder than you think!

Located in a field of 2000 sq. m., Harmony Villa is a brand new residence, actually consisted of two independent building, rented together as one Villa.

The villa is the best choice for big families, as it offers privacy and independence within your group.

Each two storey house is furnished in a modern style, covering 220 m2 in total and accommodating up to 12 guests in beds.

Layout
Harmony Villa 1

Entering the ground floor on the first building there is a spacious open plan area, consisted of the living room, indoor dinning area and a fully equipped kitchen with top quality fixtures.

The room offers direct access to a veranda, equipped with an outdoor dining table and sitting area, offering beautiful sea views and leading to the large lawn covered area that takes you to the beach.

One bedroom, also offering outdoor access, as well as a bathroom with shower cabin can also be found on this level.

Reaching the first floor, guests can find two additional bedrooms, each one with its own en-suite bathroom, opening to a spacious veranda, equipped with patio furniture, offering beautiful sea vistas.
Harmony Villa 2

The second area provides an additional living room with indoor dinning area and a fully equipped kitchen on the ground floor. The living room opens up to the lawn covered terrace, where guests can find an additional outdoor dining table and sitting area.

One bedroom, also offering outdoor access, as well as a bathroom with shower cabin can also be found on the ground level. On the first floor the two remaining bedrooms are located, both offering access to a spacious balcony overlooking the sea and the garden. Finally, a family bathroom with shower cabin is also available.

All bedrooms are air conditioned and equipped with 32’’ TVs. Three of the bedrooms feature a double each (1,60 X 2,00 cm), while the other three are equipped with two single beds each (0,90 X 2,00 cm), which can be joined together to form a king sized bed (1,80 X 2,00 cm) if you wish.
Outdoor area

The exterior of Harmony Villa is also its best feature!

The beautifully landscaped area leads to a small, private cove with small pebbles and sand, where you can enjoy swimming and sunbathing at peace, just with your loved ones. Also, a variety of facilities is also available, including:
  • Fully equipped barbecue facilities and outdoor dining areas.
  • Several furnished spots (with sun beds, umbrellas, outdoor living room and swing) for relaxing at the garden.
  • An outdoor shower.
  • Spacious lawn covered area, ideal for the kids to play.
  • Parking space inside the Villa premises.
  • Last but not least, the whole outdoor area is totally fenced, offering 100% privacy and safety to our guests!

Guests will have access and full privacy to all areas indoors and outdoors!
